## Body

The return of Abstract Truths. This series began in 2016 as a way to highlight jazz in all its many forms, which you can read about here. The selector for this installment’s dig finds musician and collage artist Ilyas Ahmed weaving a two-plus hour tapestry of sound, spanning 1964-2021. Ahmed in his own words, below. 

The following medley is comprised of old & new favorite (non-ECM) chillers. Maybe it’ll help to throw on after a long night. Maybe you woke up late and the sun is already hinting at turning into night and maybe you have a drink in hand and your getting ready to do it all over again. Maybe this mix will just help ease yr mind.

## Body

Capturing sets that slowly drift from sprawling psychedelia and ecstatic funk into punishing synth noise and ambient soundscapes, tapes of the Miles Davis septet’s 1975 tour of Japan reveal a band hell bent on terraforming its own universe. This 2-hour companion mix gathers the finest, most far-reaching moments from the circulating tapes along with a deep whiff of heady experimentation that was edited from the officially released concert recordings.

It’s easy to draw parallels between the more sinister elements of this music, of which there are many, and Miles’ personal descent into the murk – a spiral of physical and mental deterioration that ended in self-imposed exile just eight months later. Don’t fall for it. This is the muffled roar of a new world taking shape. | j erwin

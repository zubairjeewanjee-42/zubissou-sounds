## Body

Originally released in 2006, Rainbow found Boris operating at a rare point of balance: heavy (yes) but unforced and expansive without coming apart. Michio Kurihara’s guitar is central to the record’s atmosphere. Melodic and patient, his playing pushes “Rainbow,” “Rafflesia,” and “You Laughed Like a Water Mark” into a blurred zone somewhere between psych rock, slow-motion psychedelia, and late-night amplifier glow. Luminous.

Released earlier this month, Rainbow 2 extends the world of the original with two side-long pieces drawn from the long out-of-print Rainbow box set. Where the original LP still held onto song structure, Rainbow 2 leans further into spacious drone, low-end pulse, and hazy dual guitar interplay. More continuation than sequel, it deepens the suspended haze that made the original so singular.

## Body

After a whirlwind first season featuring Rosali, Jeff Parker, Steve Gunn, Steve Shelley of Sonic Youth, and Ethan Miller, Aquarium Drunkard Transmissions‘ All One Song: A Neil Young Podcast returns this spring with an all-new second season on Wednesday, April 1. In this deep dive audio experience soundtracked by original Young-inspired music by Chris Forsyth’s Coca Leaves and Pearls, liner notes author, musician, and Shakey historian Tyler Wilcox (Pitchfork, Uncut, host of Radio Free Aquarium Drunkard’s Doom and Gloom from the Tomb on dublab) invites musicians/artists/writers to discuss…one single song by Neil Young. Season two features Ira Kaplan of Yo La Tengo, Kurt Vile, Matt Sweeney of Superwolves and The Hard Quartet, James Jackson Toth, Simon Joyner, and Young collaborator Micah Nelson.  

Sponsored exclusively by hi-fi streaming service Qobuz, All One Song episodes will arrive weekly on Wednesday, exploring Shakey lore, lyrics, chords, gear, etc, offering historical background and context personal to each guest. Listen HERE. 

Additionally, the Aquarium Drunkard Transmissions team, featuring host Jason P. Woodbury and Tyler Wilcox, are coming to the Big Ears Festival March 26-29 in Knoxville, Tennessee, to present a morning show at Old City Java and Wild Love Bakery, streaming live on Instagram and YouTube in collaboration between Aquarium Drunkard, Big Ears, and Talkhouse. 

Each morning from 9-11 AM EST, Woodbury and Wilcox will discuss highlights from the previous evening and acts they plan on catching that day, as well as interview special guests as they drop in. Aquarium Drunkard Transmissions begins its Big Ears coverage on Thursday, March 26 with a special preview episode featuring Thurston Moore and Kramer. 

Big Ears 2026 features a dizzying lineup, including Robert Plant, David Byrne, Laurie Anderson, John Zorn, Flying Lotus, Pat Metheny, MJ Lenderman & the Wind, and dozens of artists who have appeared on Aquarium Drunkard Transmissions, including Richard Thompson, Alan Sparhawk of Low, Pino Palladino, Blake Mills, The Dirty Three, Shabaka, Deerhoof, Marc Ribot, Nels Cline, Julian Lage, Tim Heidecker, Bill Orcutt, BASIC, Carlos Niño, Surya Botofasina, Shahzad Ismaily, Melvin Gibbs of Harriet Tubman, Georgia Anne Muldrow, His Name is Alive, Jeff Parker, Laraaji, Mary Lattimore, William Tyler, and Yasmin Williams. 

“2026 marks the 10th anniversary of the Aquarium Drunkard Transmissions podcast,” says Jason P. Woodbury. “I couldn’t be more excited to honor that milestone with these expansions of the feed. Both our Big Ears live morning show and this new season of All One Song will showcase new dimensions of the Transmissions format, and should hint at plenty more surprises to come this year.” 

Tune into Transmissions on Thursday, March 26 for Thurston Moore and Kramer’s Big Ears preview, and tune Wednesday, April 1 for the start of All One Song season two, featuring Ira Kaplan of Yo La Tengo. Listen wherever you get podcasts via the Talkhouse Podcast Network and always direct at Aquarium Drunkard.

## Body

At first glance, one might assume an unearthed collaborative grail of Don Van Vliet crooning over Roger McGuinn’s 12-string Rickenbacker. Hey, a somewhat little known factoid is that Ry Cooder was in the Magic Band on Safe as Milk, what if the Byrds frontman had also been at the studio that day? Back to reality, Beefheart & McGuinn is the new pairing of UK/Melbourne musicians William Murray and Winter McGuinn (previously of outlets like Fur and Sunfruits, respectively). Perfect for lukewarm summer mornings just on the horizon, the duo’s inaugural offering Midtown Downtime is a comforting folk-rock assortment, intoxicating harmonic blends paired with the crispest of production. With no shortage of reference points that melded the duo’s initial friendship, there’s an overarching rootsy sway of J.J. Cale captured in the breeze. Even the briskness of the record’s four tracks clocking under the two minute mark impressively feel like more expansive, full-fledged numbers. While dropping at just the right time, Midtown Downtime is ultimately a breezy foray perfect for any setting. | m neeley
